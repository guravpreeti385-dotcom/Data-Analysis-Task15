CUSTOMER SEGMENT BUSINESS ACTIONS

1️⃣ Champions

Offer exclusive VIP discounts and early access to new products.

Launch referral reward programs to leverage word-of-mouth marketing.

Provide premium loyalty benefits and personalized premium recommendations.

2️⃣ Loyal Customers

Introduce a structured loyalty points or rewards program.

Send personalized product recommendations based on purchase history.

Upsell higher-margin or premium products through targeted campaigns.

3️⃣ At Risk Customers

Send re-engagement email campaigns with special limited-time offers.

Provide discount coupons or cashback incentives to encourage return purchases.

Conduct feedback surveys to understand dissatisfaction and improve retention.

4️⃣ Potential Loyalists

Offer bundle discounts to increase purchase frequency.

Encourage subscription or membership plans for recurring purchases.

Use targeted email marketing to build stronger engagement.

5️⃣ Others

Run awareness campaigns to introduce brand value.

Provide first-time or comeback offers to encourage purchases.

Increase engagement through newsletters and promotional emails.
